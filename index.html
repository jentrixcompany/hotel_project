Our Story

Discover the journey of Tenda Hotel - from humble beginnings to becoming Nairobi's favorite local eatery

From Humble Beginnings

Tenda Hotel started in 2008 as a small family-run eatery in Nairobi's bustling CBD. Founded by Mama Njeri and her three sons, we began with just four tables and a simple menu of traditional Kenyan dishes.

What started as a way to share our family recipes with the local community quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood spot. Word spread about our authentic flavors, generous portions, and welcoming atmosphere.

Today, we serve over 500 customers daily and have expanded to include delivery services across Nairobi. But despite our growth, we remain committed to our roots - serving homemade Kenyan food with the same love and care as when we started.
